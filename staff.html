Dr. Sung and Staff

Dr. Sung received his DDS from the University of Southern California (USC) in 1990, and completed his Endodontic Residency at USC in 1992. He has held the title of Clinical Assistant Professor at USC since 1992, and was the Director of the Advanced Selective Course for the USC School of Dentistry Endodontic Program from 1992-2012. In 2014, he completed the Implant Maxi Course at the Loma Linda University School of Dentistry Implant Continuum MaxiCourse, and has offered implant services at his office since then. Dr. Sung has been practicing in Covina since 1992. He spends his free time with his family, travelling, and is an avid golfer.
